      Jack In The Box is also the title of the United Kingdom's entry at the Eurovision Song Contest 1971, performed by the Northern Irish singer Clodagh Rodgers. (Wikipedia)

       

      According to Wikipedia:

      Quote

      This song features prominently in the Monty Python's Flying Circus episode "The Cycling Tour", in which an eccentric inventor named Mr. Reginald Gulliver (Terry Jones) believes, as a result of head trauma, that he is Clodagh Rodgers.

       

      There could be a connection with Case #002 (@LUIGI1798) where we had an extract of the Eurovision in Edinburgh (1972) and an extract of Monty Python's 'The Holy Grail'.

      Following to Ugly Kid Joe's version of "Cats In The Cradle," the producer for the single is Mark Dodson.

      During the 1980s, Dodson worked with a New York City band group, Anthrax, and produced the music album "State of Euphoria" (Not sure if this connects to Rockstar's game engine, Euphoria Physics).

       

      The studio album, "State of Euphoria," was produced in Quadradial Studios, Miami, Florida.
