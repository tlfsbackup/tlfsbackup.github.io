      - This is one of The Preacher's viddles (video riddles)

      - It was released on the Sunday of April 19, 2020

      - It is 3:39 long

       it's more worthwhile to teach someone to do something (for themselves) than to do it for them (on an ongoing basis).

      THE CLIPS: TIMESTAMPS, SOURCES, DESCRIPTIONS, INFO (click on a title to be redirected to the original video)

      CLIP #1 0:00—0:40: I Am Not In A Cult [by: Poppy]

      A girl with folded hands (Poppy, the author of the original video) says, while looking directly in the camera,

      "So many people have asked me if I'm in a cult. I'm not in a cult. Many people have asked me if [audio of a man saying "gonnaenodaethat"] is a cult leader. Gonnaenodaethat is not a cult leader. We do not believe in cults. Say it with me: I am not in a cult. Say it again: I'm not in a cult."

      This happens while the text "KIRSTY - GTAForums" appears on the bottom right of the screen, implying that this girl that's talking is represented by Kirsty. If you don't know Kirsty is one of the Forums' admins, and also a Circle O' 9 member here on the board.

      CLIP #2 0:41—0:54: It's Been A Long Time, Willie Brown [Clip from the movie: Crossroads] (starts at 1:42)

      This is when the music kicks in and so we must check the original clips to listen to their original audio.

      - Man: Y'all need a ride?

      - Girl: What you waiting for, huh?

      - Young Boy: Come on, Willie. Let's get to Fulton's Point, huh?

      - WIllie Brown (the man with the hat): What time he coming 'round?

      CLIP #3 0:54—0:56: Black Hole Image Makes History [NASA's Official Website]

      Well, just that. The first image ever captured of a black hole.

      CLIP #4 0:56—1:12: POLICE ESCORT THE MACK TO MIAMI... BUT NOT TO JAIL !!!!

      This is a weird one. It's a video from British R&B singer Mark Morrison (yes, it's his official channel) where he's in the back of a white van (we can see that from the reflection on the store window during the first seconds of the video), with a lot of police cars and men surrounding it (allegedly escorting him somewhere). At 0:06 he points the camera at this Luis Vuitton bag with this sort of medallion on it that consists of a letter 'M' or 'W' in a circle.

      CLIP #5 1:12—1:14: The Disruption of 1843 [Wikipedia]

      An image taken from the Wikipedia article about the disruption of 1843, a schism in the Church of Scotland, over the issue of the Church's relationship with the State.

      CLIP #6 1:14—1:36: James Brown: Can You See The Light? [Clip from the movie: Blues Brothers] (first part)

      Jake and Elwood Blues get out of a cop car when they start talking.

      - All I'm saying is, we gotta figure out a way to get that money, honestly.

      - Uh, that could be a problem.

      - It's like the penguin says. We gotta make that move towards redemption. We gotta go to church!

      - "We gotta make that move towards redemption; we gotta go to church!". Bullsh*t.

      - Come on.

      The scene then interrupts before the following scene where the church is viewed from afar, which we'll see later on in the video.

      CLIP #7 1:36—1:38: Rubberdoll - Fetish Nation 2009 [Wikimedia Image]

      Description: A "Rubberdoll" attending a Fetish party at Circus Disco, Hollywood, California on September 12, 2009 - Photo by Glenn Francis of www.PacificProDigital.com.

      CLIP #8 1:38—2:12: Funny Old Guy Dancing

      Not much to say here, just what the title says. He throws the canes away while dancing. Maybe there's something about the way he dances?

      CLIP #9 2:12—2:14: All Seeing Eye [PSD Underwear]

      Some underwear from the brand PSD named "All Seeing Eye". In particular, the picture in the video is the very first picture out of 4 that can be found on the product's page. Here you can find more info about this logo and its meanings and interpretations.

      CLIP #10 2:14—2:29: James Brown: Can You See The Light? [Clip from the movie: Blues Brothers] (second part - starts from 4:18)

      James Brown is 'enlightened' by the rays of the sun that enter the church through its windows. He's surrounded by a bluish aura around his body. He says "The band!" two times before the clip ends. I also noticed that there's a decoration behind him that resembles the number "9".

      CLIP #11 2:29—2:31: Aerial Video Of A Bird's Eye View Of The Traverse Theater And Usher Hall Concert Hall, Edinburgh, Scotland

      Aerial view of the Usher Hall, a famous concert hall located in Edinburgh, Scotland.

      CLIP #12 2:31—2:52: Wonka's Welcome Song (starts at 1:45)

      During the welcome song in the movie Charlie and the Chocolate Factory, the dolls start catching fire.

      CLIP #13 2:53—2:54: Black Hole Image Makes History [NASA's Official Website] again

      Exactly the same picture of the same black hole from early on in the video. 

      CLIP #14 2:55—3:15: God Is Resurrected (Clip from the movie: Dogma) (starts at 2:47)

      The clip starts exactly as the girl does the "eyelid pull" gesture.

      Jay: What the f*ck does that mean? Has everyone just gone nuts? What the f*ck happened to that guy's head? I want some...

      The Woman stands before Jay and looks him in the eyes. Jay freezes. His expression softens. The Woman slowly smiles at him, leans forward, and kisses his cheek. She exits, leaving Jay standing there, speechless. Then he faints.

      CLIP #15 3:16—3:18: 99 Ice Cream Cone

      An image (probably promotional) about a 99 ice cream cone.

      CLIP #16 3:19—3:32: Clip from the movie: Highlander (starts at 0:46)

      Here the background music starts fading out and then stops. Connor and Ramirez run along a beach. Scene set in Morar Beach, Scotland.

      CLIP #17 3:32—3:39: Thoughts While Watching Tiger King [by: Cam Bertrand] (starts at 1:14)

      A comic video by a channel named Cam Bertrand about a movie called Tiger King. In this specific clip he says: "Ohhh, this is a cult. Yeah that's a cult... Yup." with "ZELLO - GTAForums" written at the top left of the screen, similarly to what's seen at the very start of the video.
