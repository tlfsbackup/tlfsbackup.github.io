      Case #30

       

      We Gotta Get You A Woman by Todd Rundgren, from the album Runt (1970). In this video, the song is performed by a cover band called Foxes and Fossils at a restaurant called The Twisted Taco in Fayetteville, Georgia. 

       

      Quote

      The inspiration for this song's lyric is a music executive named Paul Fishkin, who managed Rundgren's pre-Nazz group Woody's Truck Stop and worked at the Bearsville label with him. He and Rundgren became good friends, and they spent much of the summer of 1969 enjoying everything Manhattan had to offer. Fishkin didn't have much luck with the ladies, which is what Rundgren wrote the song about. - Songfacts
