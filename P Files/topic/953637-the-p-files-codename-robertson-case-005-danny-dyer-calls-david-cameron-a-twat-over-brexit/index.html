      CASE #005

       

      Welcome brothers and sisters. Please take a moment and remember to be kind to one another. No thought or theory is to be ridiculed or put down. We are all equals here and thus our thoughts are all equally weighted. I feel blessed to be among you, working diligently towards our common goal. Let's begin:

       

      The Viddle:

       

      Will There Be Realistic Eastenders in GTA VI? What we have here is the post that started it all back in February.

       

      Danny Dyer was born in East London. In 2013, he took on a role in the famous British soap-opera EastEnders. As SuperDans is kind to remind us below, Danny was the voice of Kent Paul, a music-industry slimeball who took up residence on the barstools at Club Malibu. Ken Rosenberg says: "[Paul has] got his nose so far up most of Vice City's ass, if anyone knows there whereabouts of twenty keys of coke, it's this guy."

       

      Danny sure does not seem fond of David Cameron, former British PM, stating he is to blame for the United Kingdom's departure from the European Union. Mr. Cameron was off in Nice, France at the time, having an exit of his own. 

       

      The Rhyme:

       

      Quote

      So doon the rabbit hole we go

      Where it taks us

      Naebody knows 

      Tae the tops o' thi mountains

      Tae thi depths o' thi seas

      Come join thi adventure

      Tae see whit ah see

       

      We get an allusion to Lewis Carrol's 1865 novel 'Alice's Adventures in Wonderland' (you may be familiar) in this poem that invites us on a merry journey of discovery "to see what I see?" What does he see? 

       

      Translation (best of my abilities):

      So down the rabbit hole we go

      Where it takes us

      Nobody knows

      To the tops of the mountains

      To the depths of the seas

      Come join the adventure

      To see what I see
