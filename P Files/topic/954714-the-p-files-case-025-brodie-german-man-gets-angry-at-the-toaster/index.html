      There are a couple of toast based references I can see here, to toast someone is to raise a glass and celebrate an individual for an accomplishment, to refer to another as toast in slang terms infers that they are done for and that their cards are marked, finally it could be a loose reference relating to cockney rhyming slang, in the GTA London title when dying a message would pop up saying 'You're Brown Bread', which is cockney rhyming slang for dead, similarly to referring to someone as toast, this ties in with what seems like a key theme within the choice of title for the Eastenders topic.

      I feel like this video might be a metaphor for us.

      The man is desperate and angry wanting toast from his toaster, but the toaster won't make his toast and he's stuck with corn muffins. Similar to how we're desperate for news from Rockstar regarding the next GTA, but instead all we get is more GTA Online updates or event weeks.

      We are the man, Rockstar is the toaster, the toast represents GTA VI, and the corn muffins are GTA Online updates.
