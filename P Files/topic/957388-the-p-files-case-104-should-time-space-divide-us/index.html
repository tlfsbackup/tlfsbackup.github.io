      "Time and Space"
       

      I decided to look at Francis Sinclair's drawing.
      I noticed the drawing has the planet Jupiter.
      Jupiter in Greek mythology is Zeus the God of Thunder.

      His father is Cronus/Chronos/Kronos the God of Time or God of the Harvest (Preacher mentioned the word "Harvest," but don't remember where)

      Kronos is a watch brand in the GTA universe.
      The brand's motto is "The end of time"
