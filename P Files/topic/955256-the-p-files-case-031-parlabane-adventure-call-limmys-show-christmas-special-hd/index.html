      Case #031: "Adventure Call - Limmy's Show! Christmas Special!"

      Upload date: Dec 28, 2013 

      Video duration: 4:42

      Episode Aired: 22 Dec, 2013

      About Limmy´s Show!: Limmy's Show! is a Scottish anti-humour sketch show broadcast on BBC Two Scotland, written and directed by Brian Limond. Limond stars as himself and a variety of characters in a series of observational, surreal, dark and bizarre sketches. Limmy frequently breaks the fourth wall by directly talking to viewers through the camera. The show stars Brian Limond, Paul McCole, Alan McHugh, Kirstin McLean and Ryan Fletcher. The first series featured a completely different supporting cast, consisting of Debbie Welsh, Tom Brogan and Raymond Mearns. Relased in 11 January 2010 until 22 December 2013

      About Falconhoof: The humble, soft-spoken and consistently well-intentioned costumed host of "Adventure Call", a live televised call-in show in which he guides players through a fantasy role-playing game in order to win cash prizes. He regularly has to deal with angry, unexpected and sometimes misleading or threatening callers and situations.

      About Brian Limond: Brian Limond, born in 20 October 1974 in Glasglow, Scotland, better known as Limmy, is a Scottish comedian, actor, screenwriter, producer, musician, author, podcaster, and streamer. While working as a website designer and Flash developer, he began releasing comedy on his website and blog, Limmy.com, which contained various humorous Flash-based projects.In late 2006, he released a daily podcast called Limmy's World of Glasgow, which received interest from the mainstream British media.

      After continuing his comedy work for several years, Limmy was commissioned by BBC Scotland to create his own sketch show, Limmy's Show. The show ran for three series and a Christmas special between 2010 and 2013, and won two BAFTA Scotland awards. He returned to BBC Scotland with another sketch show, Limmy's Homemade Show, with a one-off episode in 2018 and a full series in 2020. Limmy has also engaged in various other pursuits, such as writing several books and performing live shows. He is a prolific user of online media platforms such as Twitter, YouTube, Twitch and previously Vine.

       

      Thanks to the brother brother and division partners D.I. @SuperDans and @Praetor_Peregrinus for the info:
       

      Quote

      The song on the intro is a song from Jean Michel Jarre called Oxygene, it's a song played on the radio station 'The Journey' within GTA IV which we all know is set in Liberty City, there have been multiple references to Liberty City within the viddles, one of the Sunday Services featured a clipshow from GTA IV, and references have been made to Jamaica within the viddles too, the radio stations name is perhaps a reference to the journey we are on, also perhaps a refence to Liberty City having a role within GTA 6

       

      Quote

      During the viddle the character portrayed by Limmy in the Adventure Call gives his listener many clues (perhaps a reference to the help in riddle form we've had to try and collect information about GTA 6) whilst believing the man on the line is in dire need of the finances, even going as far as to upset his superiors in aiding the man and subsequently costing himself in the process to the sum of £5000 by sharing the information against direct orders from his superiors, Limmy's character helps the caller to win only to find out the man is not short of money having bought his son an Audi TT for Christmas and is told by the caller that they are visiting 'Florida' on holiday on Monday, another direct refence within the viddles

      Quote

      One thing that stands out for me: of all Adventure Call’s episodes (all of them feature Jarre’s “Oxygene”), P. went it the one that mentions Florida. So Florida is a thing he’s been trying to establish.

      Original post where The Preacher posted the viddle:
       

      On a theory that I have, I think the same as SuperDans, Limmy in the show gives clues and directions to the participants to help them in their search, to be able to win the "prize" that the participants want so much.
