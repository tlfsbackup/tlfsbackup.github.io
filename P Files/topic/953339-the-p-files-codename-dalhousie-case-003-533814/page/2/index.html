      I was taking a look at this viddle again because why not. For some reason it's the one I want to solve the most, maybe because it's the first true viddle ever, the start of it all, who knows.

      Well, anyway, first I think that we should take into consideration my theory which I posted a while ago (it's actually been a month - wow). To sum it up:

      The '14' in the title, the video length (14:53) and the length of the 'pig squealing' part (41 seconds) all points to 4/1 - 1/4, April 1. In the video there's a TV noise effect that, in the UK, was called 'snow'. This video was released on March 31, and we had no idea that a day later, Rockstar would enable snow in both GTAO and RDO as part of an April Fool's Joke

      Now there's some other interesting things about this video. Now that we have a lot more of viddles like this, we can see that there are common themes which could very well have a deeper meaning to them:

      - Animals, specifically rabbits (here also a pig but I don't think there's other pigs in the other viddles)

      - The DeLorean

      - The Usher Hall (already two clips from this video are recorded there, as stated by @Kirsty in her summary video, and we've seen this place a lot of times in the viddles)

      These elements, like other more recent ones, could all have a common key, a common solution. If we find out what the DeLorean means, what it represents, then maybe, we can get not 1, but 10 steps closer to the bigger picture.

      I think this is the viddle we're the closest to finding out the meaning of, but we still don't know many things about it, like the meaning of some clips, or its very title: 533814. If 14 stands for 4/1, what do the rest of the numbers represent? We're probably still pretty far from the truth, but maybe this is a good starting point.

      So, I'm pretty sure this video is about Manhunt.

      When the other day I was playing the casino heist and saw this painting which I later posted here, this video immediately came to mind.

      I mean, it's all there: the pig, the white rabbit, and even the old TV. The DeLorean has been a recurring theme throughout The Preacher's viddles so I don't think it's related to Manhunt and to this video specifically, though there's definitely a link between Manhunt and a possible GTA 6 at this point, considering the Preacher's videos are not ALL about Manhunt and the DeLorean, as I said, appears in many circumstances.

       

      In Manhunt 1 you follow the white rabbit, just like the one in the video with the guitar in hand singing a metal cover of Feel Good.

      At 14:12 there's the infamous pig squealing, obviously another reference to Manhunt, particularly the character Piggsy

      Also, I found out that the director for Manhunt 1 is Brian Cox, and well the preacher in the video speaks to a certain "brother Cox". Yeah, it's not "cocks" in the end.

      Finally, the noise effect in the video was heavily used in Manhunt 1, though this can also be interpreted as the "snow effect" -> snow in GTA Online and Red Dead Online on April 1 for the Preacher to show his credibility. I think his riddles can often have multiple valid explanations.

      You think Rockstar wouldn't go back to a franchise like Manhunt? Well take a look at this video and similar:

      They sure weren't shy as far as gore goes in RDR2.

       

      There are still many questions yet to be answered about this. Firstly, what the rest of the numbers mean. 533814 is not random, and we know 14 means April 1/snow in Rockstar's online titles on April 1. What about the rest? Does the number have a meaning in its entirety or does it need a specific interpretation that goes further than "53 = may 3'?
